Moving Average Converngence Divergence - MACD

Parameters

  • Name: The text that identifies the indicator
  • Color: Indicator's color
  • Fast Period - The period of the fast MA
  • Slow Period - The period of the slow MA
  • Signal Period - The MA period of the signal plot
  • Average Type - The type of MA to use
  • Signal Line Color - Signal plot color
  • Oscillator Line Color - Oscillator color
