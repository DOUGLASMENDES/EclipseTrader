Tracking orders

To track the orders status, open the Orders view. On the main menu bar, click Window > Show View > Other... > EclipseTrader > Orders.

The orders view shows all the submitted orders, ordered by date and organized into category tabs:

  1. All: all orders.
  2. Pending: orders that are waiting to be executed.
  3. Filled: filled orders.
  4. Canceled: orders that were canceled either by the user or the trading provider.
  5. Rejected: rejected orders.

To cancel a pending order, right click on it and select Cancel from the popup menu, or click on the red cross icon on the view's toolbar.
